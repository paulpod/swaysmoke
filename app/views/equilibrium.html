The Equilibrium Platform

The press should act as a critical watchdog for the people, but too often today they are used as lobbying tools to support corporate or politcal agendas. That's why we are developing - Equilibrium - a news service that aggregates every side of the argument so you can cut through the biased agendas.

How it works

We take key issues, debates and questions gripping society everyday and organise the media landscape from left to right.

All national publications are included, so we retain no bias. Each issue, debate or question contains three layers.
