Features

At the heart of the service are three core features that help you navigate the campaign trail:

1. Non-Partisan Coverage of the Campaign Trail
We objectively bring together news and opinion from left and right so you can cut through the spin and propaganda, getting to the heart of the debate.

2. A Comparison of Policies not Personalities
We simplify each party's manifesto breaking it down into individual policies that you can compare against one another.

3. A System that Matches your Point of View with Political Candidates
At the end of each piece of news, policy or opinion you leave a score that determines how much you disagree or agree with what you have read.

Using an objective data modelling system we build a profile that outlines where you sit on the political spectrum determining which candidates are most aligned to your views.

As the campaign develops you can see how your opinion evolves and changes.
