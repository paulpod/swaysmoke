Democratising News & Opinion

What is it?

Sway is designed to objectively bringing together news and opinion from every side of the argument.

We follow national and local elections, grabbing news and stories from left to right, so voters can, easily and quickly understand the key issues shaping the campaign trail.

Our community gets exposed to a broad, balanced view of content allowing them to make an informed, unbiased decision about who to vote for.

About us

We believe a healthy democracy is dependant on the electorate to make educated and informed decisions. With the rise of fake news, populism in the press and the effect of social media to limit our exposure to new ideas this has never been under greater threat.

We believe that democracy can only truly thrive if the electorate is given the opportunity to read a balanced and informed spread of opinion, argument, and fact. That's why we created Sway - so the electorate can cut through the spin and propaganda and become better-informed voters.

Because a better informed electorate will ultimately make better decisions.
